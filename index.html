Clicking game


An early-access clicking game prototype.
Goal is to get a high score or combo.
Move cursor on circles and press "W".
Game speed increases over time, game is infinite.
To gain a high score, holding a combo is essential, as it gives the player exponentially more score per click as combo grows.
This game was made using the Unity game engine and C#.
Currently the game is in its early development stage, this means that all builds are experimental and may be buggy.
At this moment the gameplay is limited in functionality.

You can download the game for free.
